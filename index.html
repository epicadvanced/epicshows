About EPiC

The Teachers:

The Ensemble Production Company's (EPiC) teachers include Erik Berkowitz, Britt Block, and Peter Parish, who combine their acting and technical skillsets to craft a drama program that not only teaches the students basic theater skills, but also creates a family within it. First year drama students start in 'Beginning Drama' and work their way up to 'Advanced' where large-scale, outside of school, productions are put on that showcase the students' acting and technical capabilities that were cultivated throughout their years in the program.
